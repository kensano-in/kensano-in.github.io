Poem

Main tere baare likhta bhi hoon, koi poochhe toh mukar bhi hoon,
Is shehar mein bas itna sa hoon — na poora hoon, na idhar ka hoon.

Main har raat thoda sa marta hoon, phir subah jee leta hoon,
Kuch alfaaz se bach kar rehta hoon, kuch khud se hi katraata hoon.

— Shubh
